The K300 N

Spontaneous Fun Any Time

Designed to deliver spontaneous fun on the street, the K300 N turns up the heat and re-defines the naked segment as it offers superior ergonomics along with the thrill of a sports motorcycle. The spirited engine features a 292cc, 4-stroke, liquid-cooled system with four valves and EFI. The K300 N dominates the road with an impressive 27.5 HP and 25Nm of torque managed via a six-speed gearbox and a slipper clutch to maintain speed in corners while downshifting. K300 N also comes loaded with features such as two riding modes. Plush inverted telescopic front fork suspension and rear mono shock suspension. Front and rear disk brakes with dual-channel ABS deliver strong yet predictable stopping power. The K300 N feels at home zipping around the city or on weekend outings and extended highway joyrides.

The K300 R

Race Mode Engaged

If you are passionate about speed, then the K300 R is your perfect companion. A streamlined machine with a knack for speed and designed to keep you engaged for hours. This new model characterizes high-performance and a sleek aerodynamic design with a short windshield, compact handlebars, aesthetic bassinet frame and drag-reducing fairings to cut through the air. It features a 292cc, 4-stroke, liquid-cooled system with four valves and EFI that offers an exciting 27.5 HP and consistent 25Nm of torque. Which is more than enough to propel this 151kg racing sport machine. Sleek lines and an aggressive riding position complete the K300 R as a sports bike designed to put the show on the road.

The V302C

The Wild One

The V302C design harks back to the days of slick gelled hair, leather jacket, blue jeans and leather boots topped off with black sunglasses. This wild one is an exhilarating, lightweight and supremely comfortable custom cruiser, and perfect companion as you step away from convention, to make your own rules. An original brute that is both iconic and authentic. The V302 has a fresh, clean, contemporary sense of style and courage to take on the world. Designed with uncompromising attention to detail, the V302C is the perfect canvas for your rebel attitude and unconventional style.
